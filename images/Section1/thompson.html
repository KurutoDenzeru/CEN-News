Photos of Kit Thompson in jail following Tagaytay incident.

Author: Arnold Y. Bustamante, Tito P. Tan | March 18, 2022

- The photos of Kit Thompson behind bars following the alleged assault on his girlfriend Ana Jalandoni have gone viral
- It showed the actor already behind bars, wearing a dark shirt and in jeans
- News of the alleged assault surfaced on March 18, Friday, in the morning, after Ana allegedly sought help
- Tagaytay PNP rescued her from a hotel where she was staying

Photos taken and shared by Grace Rutao of ABS-CBN News of arrested actor Kit Thompson behind bars have gone viral.

Photo: Kit Thompson (@kitthompson)

Source: Instagram Read


He was arrested after Tagaytay PNP rescued Ana Jalandoni, Kit's girlfriend from a hotel in the area. News about the alleged assault surfaced online after Ana allegedly sought help. A screenshot of the alleged appeal for help by Ana was shared on social media.

PNP Tagaytay then came to the rescue.

According to reports, hotel staff also helped in getting Ana to be rescued.

Cornerstone Management, the talent management firm that handles the career of Kit earlier released an official statement, albeit no specific comment about the incident was made.

"We have just been informed that one of our artists, Mr. Kit Thompson, was allegedly involved in an incident that transpired last night in Tagaytay City, Cavite. As we have yet to receive a formal report regarding the alleged incident, we could not give a response nor comment on the matter.

However, we request the public to be mindful of casting judgment based on unfounded reports being circulated online."

Kit Thompson became known in showbiz after joining Pinoy Big Brother: Teen Edition 4 in 2012. He then landed several roles in showbiz in the same year.
